Tips for treating allergies8

Don’t let your seasonal sniffles keep you down.
Learn the best way to handle them

  • Police Pollen

    Know what causes your allergies and try to avoid them

  • Wear a mask

    If you have to expose yourself to your allergens then wear a mask

  • Cool and dry

    Keep your home cool and dry, dust mites thrive in homes that are warm and have humidity above 50 %

  • Wash your hair at night

    Rinse the pollen out, especially if you’re a gel or mousse fan These products can trap pollen

  • Keep your nose clean

    Pollen sticks to your nose so keep it clean at all times

  • Use barriers

    To fight dust mites, look for mattress and pillow encasements made from organic cotton

  • Boil your bedding

    You should wash your sheets and pillow cases in extremely hot water at least once a week
