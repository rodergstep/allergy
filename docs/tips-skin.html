Tips for Treating Skin Allergies11

You can do some things to make the itch more comfortable:

  • Avoid Contact

    It might sound obvious, but it’s worth a reminder. You shouldn’t use or touch what triggers your allergy.

  • Chill out

    A cool compress or shower can help calm a fiery rash. Gently pat dry and then moisturize.

  • Go Baggy

    Don’t wear tight clothes. They can irritate your rash. Play it loose and cool.

  • Add an anti–itch cream

    Use a lotion that may relieve itching.
