Antihistamines are a class (group) of drugs used in the treatment of allergic disorders and some other conditions.4

In what conditions are antihistamines used for?9

Antihistamines are commonly used:

1. To relieve the symptoms associated with hay fever.

This can include:
  • Rhinitis (inflammation of the nose)
  • Conjunctivitis (inflammation of the eyes)
  • Sneezing
  • Itching of the eyes, nose and throat

2. To reduce the severity of the rash and itching associated with nettle-type rashes such as urticaria (hives) and generalised pruritus (itch).

3. In the emergency treatment of severe allergic reactions.
